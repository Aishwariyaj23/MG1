About Us

Founded by Aishwarya, Aishaura Microgreens is rooted in a passion for nutrition, sustainability, and supporting our vibrant local community.

  • Transparent food traceability — from seed to plate

  • Boost immunity, aid digestion, enhance skin & vision
  • Support sustainable agriculture and reduce your carbon footprint
