Microgreens Avocado Toast [+]

Toast bread, mash avocado with lemon juice and salt, spread, then top with microgreens and chili flakes.

Sunflower Green Smoothie [+]

Blend sunflower microgreens with banana, mango, and coconut water for an energizing breakfast.

Microgreen Buddha Bowl [+]

Base of quinoa/rice, layered with veggies, beans & handfuls of microgreens. Top with tahini-lemon dressing.

Radish Microgreen Salad [+]

Combine radish microgreens with cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, vinaigrette & sunflower seeds.

Amaranth Paratha Rolls [+]

Add microgreens to dough, roll into paratha; fill with paneer/veggies and roll. Serve with yogurt dip.

Mustard Microgreen Raita [+]

Mix yogurt with cucumber, mustard microgreens, cumin, and salt. Chill before serving.

Broccoli Microgreen Stir-Fry [+]

Quick sauté veggies, toss in microgreens at the end with sesame and soy sauce. Serve with rice/noodles.

Pea Shoot Asian Noodle Bowl [+]

Stir-fry pea shoots with noodles, garlic, and ginger. Top with sesame oil and spring onions.
